# Client Brief

This is the raw input you bring to the grill session. It should be unrefined — the grilling exists to turn it into something precise.

---

## Example: Slack message from Sarah Chen

> Hey team — our retention numbers aren't great. Students sign up for a few lessons then drop off. I'd love to add some gamification to the platform. Points for completing lessons, maybe streaks, some kind of level system. Could help keep people engaged. Let me know what you think!
>
> — Sarah

---

## How to use this

Feed it verbatim into `/grill-me`. Don't pre-process it, don't add your own interpretation. The grill session will surface the assumptions, fill the gaps, and build the shared understanding that Sarah's message can't provide on its own.

If you have a meeting transcript or a call recording, run it through a transcription tool first and feed that instead.
